CRADY Account Deletion

CRADY users can request deletion of their account and related data.

How to delete your account

Open the CRADY app and follow these steps:

  1. Open the CRADY app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

If the in-app delete account feature is not available, you may request deletion by email.

Email: forgeapps.lab@gmail.com

Subject: CRADY Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address used to log in to CRADY.

Data that will be deleted

  • CRADY account information
  • Watchlist data
  • Portfolio data
  • App data linked to your CRADY account

Data retention

After account deletion, account-related data will be deleted from our active systems. Some records may be retained only where required by law, security, fraud prevention, or legitimate operational purposes.

Contact

For account deletion or privacy questions, contact us at:
forgeapps.lab@gmail.com

계정 삭제 방법

CRADY 앱에서 아래 순서로 진행해 주세요.

  1. CRADY 앱을 실행합니다.
  2. 설정으로 이동합니다.
  3. 회원 탈퇴를 선택합니다.
  4. 삭제 요청을 확인합니다.
