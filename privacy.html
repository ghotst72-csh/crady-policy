Privacy Policy

English

CRADY provides dividend calendar, portfolio management, watchlist, and ETF analysis features.

Information Collected
- Email address used for login authentication
- Portfolio holdings entered by the user
- Watchlist and app preferences

Purpose
- User authentication
- Portfolio and watchlist synchronization
- Personalized ETF analysis and dividend information

Data Storage
- User account and portfolio information may be stored securely using Supabase services

Ads & Analytics
- This app may use Google AdMob advertising services
- Device information may be collected for advertising and analytics purposes

Data Sharing
- Personal information is not sold to third parties
